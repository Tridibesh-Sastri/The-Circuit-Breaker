About The Circuit Breaker Electronics Club

The Circuit Breaker Electronics Club is a passionate community of students driven by curiosity and innovation in electronics and embedded systems. We provide hands-on experience, organize impactful events, and foster peer-to-peer learning.

Whether you're just beginning your electronics journey or you're an experienced tinkerer, our club is a place to experiment, collaborate, and create projects that make a difference.

  • Build real-world electronic projects
  • Host and participate in workshops and hackathons
  • Collaborate on research and innovation
  • Learn from industry experts and alumni
